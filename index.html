About Us.

Welcome to Blockchain Africa. This is a group birthed to help each other grow and become financially independent. With this in mind, this group shall offer :

1

Blockchain lectures and introduction to other blockchain platforms.

2

Enquiries and complaints on blockchain related topics.

3

Blockchain latest news and updates to keep all members up-to-date with the latest happenings.

4

Lectures for newbies and oldbies who are yet to familiarise themselves on how the blockchain works.

At Block.Africa We develop your skills in Blockchain Technology, Cryptocurrency and a whole lot more.
